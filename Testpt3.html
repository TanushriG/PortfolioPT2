About me!

I'm Tanushri Goyal, an artist with experience in multiple mediums including acrylic, oil pastel, and pencil. I have taken a keen interesting in art since I was in elementary school and started formally learning art when I was 11. I am eager to build on my artistic knowledge and continue creating art.
